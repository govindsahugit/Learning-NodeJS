Different Ways to Receive Payments with Razorpay

1. Payment Links
    - Create a unique link to send via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or social.
    - Customer opens the link → pays via UPI, card, wallet, etc.
    - Best for freelancers, small shops, or one‑time payments.

2. Payment Pages
    - Prebuilt mini‑site in Razorpay (no coding).
    - Add logo, product details and pricing; customers pay on the page.
    - Good for “Buy tickets”, “Donate now” or product pages.

3. Payment Buttons
    - Simple “Pay Now”/“Donate” button to embed on your site/blog.
    - Clicking opens Razorpay checkout.
    - Ideal for lightweight sites without a backend.

4. Checkout (Integrated)
    - Embed Razorpay’s checkout into your app/website so users stay on your site.
    - Supports UPI, cards, wallets, net banking, EMI.
    - Best for professional e‑commerce experiences.

5. QR Codes
    - Generate UPI QR codes customers scan with Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.
    - Amount can be fixed or open.
    - Great for shops, restaurants and offline POS.

6. Subscriptions
    - Recurring billing for monthly/annual plans.
    - Razorpay auto‑collects payments for SaaS, memberships, gyms.

7. Invoices
    - Create professional invoices with customer name, amount and due date.
    - Invoice includes a payment link so customers can pay directly.

8. Smart Collect (Virtual Accounts & UPI IDs)
    - Assign unique virtual bank accounts or UPI IDs per customer.
    - Razorpay auto‑tracks and reconciles incoming transfers.
    - Ideal for bulk or enterprise collections.
